Top Attractions in Kanyakumari

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

This iconic monument located on a small island offers spiritual tranquility and panoramic views.

Thiruvalluvar Statue

Standing tall beside the memorial, this statue honors the great Tamil poet and philosopher Thiruvalluvar.

Kanyakumari Temple

Dedicated to Goddess Kanya Kumari, the temple is an important spiritual destination for pilgrims.
